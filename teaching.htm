Teaching

Undergraduate courses at the Computer Engineering and Informatics Department of Patras University:

  • "Algorithmic Foundations of Sensor Networks" (from 2007)
  • "Probability" (from 2002)
  • "Probabilistic Techniques" (from 2002)

Courses at the Postgraduate Program "Computer Science and Engineering", offered by the Department of Computer Engineering and Informatics of Patras University:

  • Advanced Algorithmic Aspects of Ad Hoc Networks (from 2007)
  • "The Probabilistic Method as a Tool in Computer Science - Randomized Algorithms" (from 2004)

For 2000 - 2001, teaching of the course "The Probabilistic Method" in the Interdepartmental Postgraduate Program in "Mathematics of Computers and Decision Making", of the Departments of Mathematics and of Computer Engineering and Informatics of the University of Patras.

For 2002 - 2003, Teaching of the course "The Probabilistic Method" of the "Postgraduate Program in Logic, Algorithms and Computation", sponsored by the Departments of Mathematics, Informatics and M.I.TH.E. (Methodology, History and Theory of Science), of the University of Athens, by the General Sciences Department and the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering of the National Technical University of Athens and by the Department of Computer Engineering and Information of the University of Patras (http://www.math.uoa.gr/~mpla/).

During the past years, teaching of:

  • "Operating Systems"
  • "Advanced Algorithms"

Also, supervision of more than 30 MSc and Diploma Theses so far.
